Welcome!

We are building a community of open source geospatial software users at LSU. We meet every Wednesday at 1pm in the Student Union Room 312 (Council Room). Anyone is welcome to join! If you are interested in presenting, or have a topic you would like to learn more about, checkout the presentations page. We don't have presentations every time, but it's nice to be able to see what everyone is up to.

{% include schedule_table.html %}

Get Involved

To get announcements about upcoming meetings and more, join our LSU listserv. Send an email to listserv@listserv.lsu.edu with this text for the body: SUBSCRIBE geoforall_lsu <Your Name>. No subject line is necessary.

We also have a public-facing discussion forum for sharing resources that anyone is welcome to join.
