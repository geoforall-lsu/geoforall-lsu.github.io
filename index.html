Welcome!

We are building a community of open source geospatial software users at LSU. Anyone is welcome to join, just get on our mailing list to get details ahead of each meeting.

If you are interested in presenting, or have a topic you would like to learn more about, checkout the presentations page where you can sign up or suggest a topic. Presentations are very informal.

We've also started recording our meetings, and will be posting the videos below.

{% include schedule_table.html %}

Get Involved

To get announcements about upcoming meetings and more, join our LSU listserv. Send an email to listserv@listserv.lsu.edu with this text for the body: SUBSCRIBE geoforall_lsu <Your Name> (example: SUBSCRIBE geoforall_lsu Buster Keaton). No subject line is necessary.

We also have a public-facing discussion forum for sharing resources that anyone is welcome to join.
